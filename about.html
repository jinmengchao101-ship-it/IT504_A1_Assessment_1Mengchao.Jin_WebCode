About Us

Harakeke Studio is a creative web design studio based in New Zealand. We focus on building modern, clean and effective digital experiences for small businesses, freelancers, and startups.

Our Mission

To help businesses build strong online presence through simple, beautiful and user-friendly websites.

Our Vision

We aim to make professional web design accessible to everyone, not just big companies.

Our Team

A small team of designers and developers passionate about creativity, design, and technology.

Why Choose Us?

We combine design, development and marketing knowledge to deliver websites that not only look good but also perform well.
